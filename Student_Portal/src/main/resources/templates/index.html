About Us

It is the outcome of visionary mission of Dr. D. Y. Patil, Founder President of Dr. D. Y. Patil Pratishthan. The Institute has been set up to fulfill the following objectives: To nurture and develop skilled manpower in the field of IT and Electronics. To impart courses with practical-centric approach. To provide quality and latest information in the field of technology. The core strength of the institute lies in the dynamic management team, excellent infrastructure, experienced and qualified faculties who have been trained to deliver the latest and most advanced technological knowledge to the students. Industry experts visit our institute from time to time and deliver lectures. The Institute is also involved in other activities such as software solutions, product development, network services etc, which supports the IT industry. In the future, there are plans to expand the areas of services offered by the Institute. The Institute is located in a tranquil environment within walking distance to the Akurdi railway station. All the necessary amenities are provided to the students to enable them to work in the institute round the clock. The institute is open 24x7 and free wi-fi internet access is provided to students throughout the campus.



Our Mission

  • Our mission is to create high skilled IT resources which are recognized by major corporates in India. We strive to inculcate strong technical and interpersonal skills that help the students excel in the corporate world.
